# LADAR

**LADAR — Low Atmospheric Data Acqisution and Research
A modular, drone-based platform for high-resolution measurement of the lower atmosphere, combining physics-based modeling and machine learning to reconstruct 3D environmental fields from sparse measurements.

---

## 🌟 Objectives

- Develop a modular drone system for high-resolution lower-atmosphere characterization  
- Integrate physics-informed modeling with machine learning for volumetric field reconstruction  
- Enable studies of thermal gradients, turbulence, pollutant dispersion, and transient events  
- Provide a versatile platform for both fundamental atmospheric physics and applied environmental research

---

## ⚙️ Development Strategy

- **Scalable modular architecture**: Start with core stack, expand as research grows  
- **Adaptive & autonomous sensing**: Real-time anomaly detection and dynamic sampling  
- **Flexible instrumentation**: Supports temperature, humidity, pressure, IMU, GPS, air quality, flow sensors, and thermal imaging
